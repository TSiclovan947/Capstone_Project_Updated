Meet Our Founders

Therapy For You was founded by Nathan Howard, Michael Solomon, and Tabitha Siclovan in 2018. T4U is an online therapy company that seeks to provide easy access to proper and beneficial therapy that is not only accessible but affordable. We use direct messaging, webcam, among others to provide counseling and therapy from anywhere.

Nathan Howard had body image issues that lead to depression when he was younger but found his outlet of fitness. He wants others to find their outlet and thus helped to found therapy for you.


Tabitha Siclovan is the ninth child out of all eleven biological children. She decided to co-found this therapy business because she herself has gone through hard times with the recent passing away of her mother.


Michael Solomon likes to bake cakes and cookies for people He loves nature especially at night because of the wonderful stars. He really enjoys art and video games. He wanted to bring his creativity into the therapy business.
