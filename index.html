Our Vision

To provide a path to healing, while finding peace and tranquility in the midst of life’s storms through quality and accessible health care.

Our Company Goals

We will strive to provide an affordable alternative to regular in-person therapy that will be just as beneficial and help people find a place in life that will make them feel like they have a purpose, that their life matters, and be able to help them on that journey. We hope to partner with companies that will help these patients in their future (with jobs, etc.). However, our main goal is to help people become healthy at the level of the mind because when people are healthy at the level of the mind, the country and the world are also healthier and we can reach our biggest goal of making the world a better place.

Our Values & Principles

We at Therapy For You strive to be people of integrity. We always put our patients FIRST and train our employees to properly treat and converse with patients. Our therapists are all certified therapists. We have some volunteer certified therapists who just want to help as well.
